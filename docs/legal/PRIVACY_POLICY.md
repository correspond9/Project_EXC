## 5. Data Sharing

We share personal data only where necessary:

- **Binance:** Order execution data is sent to Binance for live trade routing. Binance's privacy policy applies to data processed on their side.
- **KYC/AML Providers:** Identity documents are shared with our accredited KYC provider for verification.
- **Regulatory Authorities:** We are required to share data with VARA, UAE Central Bank, and law enforcement agencies upon valid legal request.
- **No sale of data:** We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Passwords stored using bcrypt hashing (never in plaintext)
- JWT-based authentication with short-lived tokens (15 minutes)
- Access controls: all staff access to user data is logged and audited
- Regular penetration testing
