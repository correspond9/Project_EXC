## Important Notice

**Please read this Risk Disclosure Statement carefully before using the XChange Platform for live trading. This document describes the material risks associated with virtual asset trading.**

**Virtual asset trading is highly speculative. You may lose your entire investment.**

---

## 1. Market Risk

Virtual asset prices are extremely volatile and can change rapidly and unpredictably. Factors that may affect prices include:
- Regulatory announcements or changes in law
- Technological developments or security breaches
- Market sentiment, media coverage, and social media activity
- Liquidity conditions and large market participant activity
- Macroeconomic events

Past performance is not indicative of future results.

---

## 2. Leverage Risk

Leveraged futures trading amplifies both potential gains and potential losses.

A position with 10x leverage means a 10% adverse price move will result in a 100% loss of your margin. Your position may be **liquidated** automatically if your margin balance falls below the maintenance margin requirement. You may lose your entire margin deposit.

**We strongly advise inexperienced traders to avoid leveraged trading.**

---

## 3. Liquidity Risk

There may be situations where you are unable to close a position at a desired price due to insufficient market liquidity. During periods of high volatility, slippage between the quoted price and the execution price may be significant.

---

## 4. Technology & Operational Risk

- The Platform may experience downtime, technical errors, or connectivity issues
- Internet connectivity failures on your side may prevent order management
- Exchange API disruptions (Binance) may delay or prevent order execution
- Software bugs could affect order routing or balance calculation

We are not liable for losses arising from technical failures beyond our reasonable control.

---

## 5. Counterparty Risk

Live orders are routed to Binance. Your funds on Binance are subject to Binance's terms, security practices, and regulatory status. In the event of a Binance insolvency or regulatory action, your funds held on Binance may be at risk.

---

## 6. Regulatory Risk

The regulatory landscape for virtual assets is rapidly evolving. Future regulatory changes in the UAE, or the jurisdictions where your underlying assets are held, may adversely affect:
- Your ability to trade
- The value of your assets
- The operation of the Platform

---

## 7. Cybersecurity Risk

- Your account could be compromised if your credentials are stolen
- Phishing attacks may attempt to steal your login information
- We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share your password or API keys with anyone

---

## 8. Tax Risk

Virtual asset transactions may have tax implications under UAE law and/or the laws of your country of residence. You are solely responsible for understanding and complying with your tax obligations. We do not provide tax advice.

---

## 9. No Investment Advice

Nothing on the XChange Platform constitutes investment advice, financial advice, or any recommendation to buy or sell any virtual asset. You should seek independent professional advice before making any trading decisions.
